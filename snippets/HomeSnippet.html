Guntur Sanam Chilli

Guntur Sannam is a variety of Chilli that grows in districts of Guntur, Warangal and Khammam. It is the most famous type of red and hot chilli with a thick skin and is in demand all over the world. “Sannam” in Telgu means “Thin and Long”.

Assam Muga Silk

Muga Silk is a variety of silk, greatly patronized by the Ahom Dynasty back in the day, and still loved by people all over the world. Known for durability, the silk is primarily produced in Assam and has been geographically tagged to the state.

Bihar Sikki Grass Work

Sikki grass work is created with a golden fiber derived from drying the Sikki grass and cutting off its flower head. This special grass is indigenous to the region, where the ancient practice had been passed down through generations.
