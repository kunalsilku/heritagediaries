    We are Heritage Diaries

    Heritage Diaries is an initiative directed towards reinvograting the rich cultural heritage of our country. From days immeomorial, our country was known far and wide for its handicrafts and agricultural produce. All states of our country have some or the other uniqueness. our effort is to bring to you all such heritages. We have tried to present very precise literature about each item so as to give you a fair idea of the handicraft. We also bring you the processes through which these items are made. In the backend we are supported by host or artisans who bring to you the rich cultural heritage of our country.

    But this is not all. We have also added an e-commerce initiatve to support all our artisans. You can visit our online store and purchase any handicraft or agricultutal product.Our robust mechanism ensures timely and damage free delivery.

    This unique initiave has been brought to you by Endesoft Private Limited who is our parent organisation. Visit endesoft webpage for more such products.
